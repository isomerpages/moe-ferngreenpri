<h4>MOE Service Excellence Award (MSEA) 2025</h4>
<p>The MSEA recognizes MOE staff who are:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Passionate in their service delivery e.g. care and respect towards students/staff/parents/stakeholders,

<p>Consistent in serving internal and external customers with C.A.R.E (Collaborate,
Anticipate, Reach out, Empathise) and</p>

<p>Inspire their colleagues to do likewise.</p>
