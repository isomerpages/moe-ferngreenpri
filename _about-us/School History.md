<h4><strong>School Official Opening</strong></h4>
<p>Fern Green Primary School started operations in 2018 and was officially
opened by Mr Gan Thiam Poh, Grassroots Advisor to Ang Mo Kio GRC GROs,
on 30 August 2021.</p>
