<p>The ICT Department seeks to align our programmes and strategies with MOE's
EdTech Masterplan for 2030 and our school's Vision and Mission.</p>
<p><strong><u>ICT Department Vision</u></strong>
</p>
<h4><strong>Innovate. Collaborate. Transform (I.C.T)</strong></h4>
<p>The ICT Department aims to:</p>
<h4><strong>Innovate</strong></h4>
<p>Create a digitally-empowered environment encouraging students and teachers
to explore and create using technology.</p>
<h4><strong>Collaborate</strong></h4>
<p>Facilitate meaningful collaborations among students, staff, and stakeholders
leveraging technology.</p>
<h4><strong>Transform&nbsp;</strong></h4>
<p>Transform learning through technology, integrating digital literacy and
technological skills.</p>
