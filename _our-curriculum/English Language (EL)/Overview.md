<p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="EL 2024" src="/images/English/English_Language_Committee.jpg"></div><h3><strong>Overview</strong></h3><p>The English Department has a vision to develop our students to be Communicative and Confident Learners.&nbsp;We want our students to have a strong foundation and be proficient in the English Language so that they can communicate confidently and effectively in Standard English for different purposes, audiences and contexts.&nbsp;Activities and programmes are structured to provide students with the confidence, purpose and enjoyment in a wide range of contexts and to convey and discover information.</p><h3><strong>Vision</strong></h3><p>Every Student A Discerning Reader, A Confident Speaker, A Creative Writer and A Critical Inquirer.</p><h3><strong>Mission</strong></h3><p>To build a strong foundation in the use of the English Language in Fern Green students through rich language learning experiences.</p>
