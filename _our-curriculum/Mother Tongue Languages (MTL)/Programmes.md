<p><strong>Cultural Exposure Programme Scheme</strong>
<br>Watching MTL drama in a theatre provides students with a unique and immersive
educational experience that enhances their understanding of literature,
performance arts, and human emotions. The live setting allows them to observe
the nuances of acting, staging, and production design in real-time, fostering
a deeper appreciation for the creative process and the collaborative effort
involved in bringing a story to life. Our school has learning journeys
to a theatre that exposes students to diverse cultural perspectives and
historical contexts, enriching their social and cultural awareness.</p>

<p><strong>Reading Programme</strong>
<br>The department is committed to develop reading habit among our students.
The Reading Programme commences in Term 2, where pupils engage themselves
in critical thinking and get fascinated by the wonders in the fictional
and non-fictional world. Silent reading takes place on Thursday and Friday
before flag raising.</p>

<p><strong>Festive Celebrations</strong>
<br>Festive celebrations are ways that students can gain better understanding

<p><strong>Speech and Drama</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The speech and drama classes are designed to give children the opportunity
